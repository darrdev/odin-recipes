Beef Brisket

Description

The brisket is seared in a hot skillet, then topped with sliced onions and garlic, then braised for about 6 hours for a tender, juicy roast. It's so worth the wait!

Corned beef is a beef brisket (a cut of meat from the breast or lower chest) that has been cured in a salt solution. "Corning" results in intensely flavorful, super tender meat. In this recipe, the corned beef brisket is seared in a hot skillet and then braised all day with garlic and onions for a melt-in-your-mouth texture and perfect flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 flat cut corned beef brisket
  • 1 tablespoon browning sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 135 degrees Celcius
  2. Discard any flavouring packet from corned beef. Brush brisket with browning sauce on both sides.
  3. Heat vegtable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and brown brisket on both sides in the hot oil, 5 to 8 minutes per side.
  4. Place brisket on rack set in a roasting pan. Scatter onion and garlic slices overs brisket and add water to roasting pan. Cover pan tightly with aluminium foil.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven until meat is tender, about 6 hours.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
